About Me

Mohammad Mozafari is an experimental multimedia artist whose practice spans animation, theater-based video production, painting, illustration, sculpture, and conceptual video art. His work explores emotional states, subconscious narratives, and the tension between personal identity and collective experience through surrealist symbolism and scientific precision.
