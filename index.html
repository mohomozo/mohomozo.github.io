About

Mohammad Mozafari is an experimental multimedia artist working across animation, theater-based video production, painting, and sculptural forms. His practice focuses on deformation, transformation, and animation as an autonomous entity.

Ongoing Projects

Current works in progress focusing on morphing, ritual movement, and non-narrative animation structures.
