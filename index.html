Mohammad Mozafari

Mohammad Mozafari is an experimental multimedia artist whose practice spans animation, painting, illustration, theater-based video production and conceptual video art.

His work explores emotional states, subconscious narratives, and the tension between personal identity and collective experience.

Artist Statement

My work operates at the intersection of emotion, memory, and experimental form. I move freely between mediums because each material reveals a different layer of meaning.

I build hybrid visual languages that invite viewers to feel ideas before fully understanding them.

Approach & Process

I approach each project as an open experiment. Improvisation, intuition, and material exploration guide my process.

Accidents and unexpected outcomes often become the core of the final work.
